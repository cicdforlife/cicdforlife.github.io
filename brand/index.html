The wordmark and sticker kit for CI/CD for Life. The identity is CRT phosphor; printed stickers use a darker green that survives a laser printer. Full usage notes are in brand/README.md.

Stickers — print-only CRT green

sticker-print-horizontal.png · sticker-print-stacked.png
For physical stickers only — not the digital brand. Badge is CRT green #0B6E3F with white CI/CD (a knockout — bare paper, no toner, so it prints crisper). Flat + dithered, no bloom: the glow is a gradient, and gradients band on laser printers. 300 DPI, no alpha.
